Tyler Kaulbars & Tara Mulvenna, Owners

Tyler Kaulbars is not the kind of arborist who manages from behind a desk. With over 25 years in the industry and seven professional certifications, he is one of the most credentialed tree care professionals working in Sarasota County today.

He is a Florida native who grew up in the trade and built Arborist Art on the principle that tree care done right is an art form. When Tyler walks your property, he sees what other crews miss. Structural risks, disease progression, canopy conflicts, root zone concerns, and the long-term health of every tree on your grounds. That level of assessment comes from decades of hands-on experience, not a weekend certification course.

Tyler is personally on-site at your community, leading assessments, directing crews, and making the calls that protect your trees and your board's liability. When your board needs someone at the table for a presentation, a proposal review, or a storm response plan, Tyler is there. He is the license holder, the lead arborist, and the face of every job Arborist Art takes on.

He is also an Approved Continuing Education Provider for Community Association Managers, which means the person caring for your community's trees is literally qualified to train the professionals who manage them.

The Operation Behind the Arborist

Behind every great field operation is someone making sure the business side runs just as sharp. That is Tara Mulvenna.

Tara brings a BA in Psychology and an MBA to a business where most competitors are winging it with a clipboard and a prayer. She manages all client communication, serves as the primary point of contact for property managers, CAMs, and accounts payable, and leads every large contract proposal Arborist Art submits.

Scheduling across multiple crews and projects, real-time adjustments for weather and priority jobs, detailed invoicing that matches your contract terms exactly, clean financial reporting, and payroll management all run through Tara. She built the systems that allow Arborist Art to operate at a level most tree companies never reach.

For property managers, this means something specific: when you work with Arborist Art, you get a vendor whose back office matches the professionalism of their fieldwork. Your proposals are detailed and accurate. Your invoices are clean. Your communication is proactive. Your board gets documentation they can actually use.

Tyler is the best arborist your community will ever hire. Tara is the reason everything around him runs like clockwork.
