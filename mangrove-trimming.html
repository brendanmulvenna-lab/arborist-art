Mangrove Trimming in Florida is Not a DIY Job

Florida mangroves are protected under state law. Trimming them without a certified professional can result in fines up to $10,000 per violation. Every cut must follow strict state guidelines on height, percentage of canopy removed, and species-specific rules.
