Not Every Tree Can Be Saved

Dead trees, diseased trees, storm-damaged trees, trees too close to structures. There are real reasons a tree needs to come down. The difference is who makes that call.

At Arborist Art, every removal decision is made by a Certified Arborist with 25 years of experience. Tyler Kaulbars personally evaluates every tree before recommending removal. If it can be saved through pruning or treatment, he will tell you. If it needs to come down, he will explain exactly why and oversee the entire removal process.

We also provide ongoing tree management programs and certified mangrove trimming for communities across Sarasota.

Tree Removal Services for Communities

Hazardous Tree Removal

Trees threatening structures, power lines, roadways, or resident safety. We assess the risk, secure permits, and execute the removal safely under certified arborist supervision.

Dead and Diseased Tree Removal

Dead trees are a liability for your community. We identify, document, and remove dead and diseased trees before they become an emergency.

Large Tree Removal

Complex removals requiring cranes, rigging, or specialized equipment. Our crew has the training and equipment to handle removals of any size in tight community spaces.

Stump Grinding

Every removal includes the option for complete stump grinding below grade. No leftover stumps creating trip hazards or pest problems in your common areas.
